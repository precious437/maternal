Neural Diagnostics (AI)

Understanding patient telemetry synchronization and automated YOLO structural analyses.

Telemetry Sync (Vital Signs)

Inputs must be numerical formats only. All fields (MHR, FHR, BP, SpO2) are required to build an accurate predictive clinical baseline before anomaly calculations begin.

Structural Detection Engine

Drag and drop standardized Ultrasound scans or multi-slice DICOM files. The active YOLOv8 model evaluates boundaries for anomalies such as placenta previa and cranial deformities.

Analytical Output

Processed scans render boundary boxes with high-confidence percentages. These logs are simultaneously backed up in the History archive for post-operative audit.

Intelligent Diagnostic Reports

Systematic archival of patient sessions and automated post-operative clinical summaries.

The Intelligent Audit Engine (located below the Surgical AI scanner) ensures every pixel segment and clinical finding is permanently tethered to the patient's record for long-term monitoring.

  • Timeline Snapshots: Every ultrasound upload triggers a clinical snapshot. These can be revisited instantly via the 'Diagnostic Archive' sidebar.
  • Telemetry Synchronization: Patient vitals (BP, Fetal Heart Rate) are automatically paired with their respective image scans for a holistic diagnostic view.
  • One-tap Restoration: Selecting a record from the history panel instantly restores the AI detections and visual markers directly onto the diagnostic terminal.
